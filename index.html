F.A.Q

  • How can I contact the organizers of the AAHVAAN Sports Fest?

    You can contact the organizers of the Sports Fest through the official website or social media pages. Additionally, you can also find contact information in the registration process.

  • What is the date of AAHVAAN2K23 ?

    The Sports Fest will be held on 23 Jan to 25 Jan at ECB Campus. Please check the official website or social media pages for updates on the event schedule and location.

  • What are the rules and regulations for the Sports Fest?

    The rules and regulations for the Sports Fest will be provided to participants during the registration process. Participants are expected to follow the rules and regulations of the event at all times.

  • Are there any prizes for the winners of the Sports Fest?

    Yes, the Sports Fest will have prizes for the winners of the different sports events. The prize will be announced on the official website or social media page.

  • What types of food will be available at the food stalls?

    The food stalls at the Sports Fest will offer a variety of food options, including snacks, meals, and drinks. There may be options such as fast food, street food, and local delicacies.
